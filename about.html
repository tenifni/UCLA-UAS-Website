About UAS

UAS is a club for all those interested in aerospace engineering, astrophysics, star gazing, or the exploration of our universe.

Meetings are held on every EVEN Wednesday at 7:30 pm (during daylight savings time) in Knudsen 2-222. They are held at 6:30 pm during pacific standard time.

We have activities every week which give you an opportunity to socialize and cooperate with others who share the same interests as you. We run a sky show after the meetings at ~9 pm on top (9th floor) of the Math Science building with the 14 in. Schmidt-Cassegrain telescope.

You can join our Facebook group or follow us on Twitter.

To be added to the mailing list or for general questions about our club, please email us at ucla.astro.club@gmail.com.
