What's UAS?

UAS is an exciting club where we discover the wonders of space, participate in build activities, go on trips, and hang out as a community. Students of all majors are welcome!

Weekly club events

Planetarium Show

Wednesday evening public planetarium shows (free and suitable for all ages) are given by current astronomy graduate students. Content varies with presenters, but commonly includes discussion about the current night sky, constellations, astronomical phenomena, and more!
Time & location: 8:00pm Wednesday, 8th floor Math Sciences building.

Show schedule

Telescope Viewing

On clear nights, visitors are invited to look through UCLA's 14" Telescope presented by undergrad UAS telescope operators. We view a variety of celestial objects, including planets, nebulae, and/or star clusters (depending on what objects are currently in the sky).
Time & location: 9:00pm Wednesday, 9th floor Math Sciences building roof.
